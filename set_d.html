PART – A    (10 × 2 = 20 Marks)

Answer All Questions – All Questions are Compulsory

1. State the number of atoms per unit cell, coordination number, and atomic radius formula for FCC and BCC structures.
2. Compare Czochralski method and Vapor Phase Epitaxy — state one key difference in each of: (i) process, (ii) crystal quality.
3. Define second moment of area (I) for a beam. Why does placing a beam with its larger dimension vertical increase its resistance to bending?
4. A rod is twisted by a couple C through an angle θ. Define torsional rigidity and write its SI unit.
5. Define SHM. State two differences between free oscillations and forced oscillations.
6. What is logarithmic decrement in damped oscillations? How is it used to measure damping?
7. What is total internal reflection? State the condition and give one practical application.

9. What is meant by normalisation of a wave function? Why must ∫|ψ|²dx = 1?
10. State the de Broglie relation. Calculate the wavelength of a 1 kg ball moving at 10 m/s and comment on why we do not observe wave nature for macroscopic objects.
